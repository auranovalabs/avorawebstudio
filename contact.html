Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process?

expand_more

We begin with a details consultation where we outline layout structures and copywriting parameters. Once finalized, we enter building and validation stages to ensure high performance and SEO readiness before deploying to production.

Do you build static sites?

expand_more

Yes, we exclusively build optimized static sites using HTML, Tailwind CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript. By avoiding heavy database-driven CMS platforms like WordPress or Shopify, we ensure site speed remains exceptionally fast, highly secure, and virtually maintenance-free.

What is the expected timeline?

expand_more

A standard site (under 5 pages) typically takes 2 to 3 weeks from kickoff to deployment. Larger or more custom layouts take between 4 to 6 weeks, depending on integration complexity.
